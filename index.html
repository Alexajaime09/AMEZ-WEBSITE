About us

Mision

At Clean Solutions, we are dedicated to providing exceptional and sustainable cleaning services in Montreal, committed to excellence, attention to detail, and environmental care. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for our clients and their employees by creating clean, healthy, and productive environments using eco-friendly products and sustainable practices.

Vision

To be the leading cleaning company in Montreal, recognized for our commitment to sustainability, innovation, and service excellence. We aspire to set new standards in the cleaning industry, becoming the trusted partner for companies that value quality and environmental responsibility.

Philosophy

We believe that a clean environment not only enhances the aesthetics of a space but also significantly contributes to the well-being and productivity of people. Our philosophy is based on:


  • Sustainability: We adopt eco-friendly cleaning practices that minimize environmental impact, using biodegradable products and efficient resource consumption techniques
  • Quality: We strive to exceed our clients' expectations in every project, paying attention to every detail to ensure impeccable results.
  • Innovation: We implement the latest technologies and methods in cleaning to offer more effective and efficient solutions.
  • Responsibility: We commit to operating ethically and transparently, prioritizing the safety and well-being of our employees and clients.

Values

  • Excellence: We are dedicated to providing the highest quality services in every job we perform.
  • Respect: We treat our clients, employees, and the environment with the utmost respect and consideration.
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty, transparency, and respect in all our interactions.
  • Teamwork: We foster a collaborative work environment to achieve the best results for our clients.
  • Customer Focus: We prioritize our clients' needs, ensuring their satisfaction and building long-lasting relationships.
